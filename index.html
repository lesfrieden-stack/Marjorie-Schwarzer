Biography & Education

Marjorie Schwarzer is a prominent museum studies educator, author, and critic. She served as the founding Administrative Director of the Museum Studies graduate program at the University of San Francisco (USF) and chaired the Department of Museum Studies at John F. Kennedy University for over 15 years.

Throughout her career, she has held educational leadership positions at the Boston Children's Museum, the Chicago Children's Museum, and the Museum of Science and Industry in Chicago, dedicating over three decades to training future generations of museum professionals.
